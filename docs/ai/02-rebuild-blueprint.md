Final Rebuild Blueprint
1. Final Recommendation
Build a new Next.js + Vercel site and keep WordPress live only until launch.

This is not a design refresh. It is a cleanup of a damaged local SEO and conversion asset. The current WordPress site has demo copy, broken links, wrong emergency language, unconfirmed bird care, Catcare confusion, index bloat, WooCommerce clutter, and tracking uncertainty. Fixing WordPress would still leave a fragile Elementor/plugin stack.

Next.js + Vercel gives Francis Petcare a clean, fast, controlled local SEO site with fewer moving parts, better tracking, cleaner schema, safer redirects, and stronger mobile conversion.

6. Stronger Launch Page Briefs
Use this copy direction across the site:

Replace generic “quality compassionate care” with specific proof.
Say “Richmond, BC” naturally on every priority page.
Say “call ahead” for urgent needs.
Say “confirmed species” instead of vague exotic claims.

SEO Title: Animal Hospital & Exotic Pet Vet in Richmond, BC | Francis Petcare Hospital

7. Conversion Architecture
Primary conversion is phone call, especially on mobile.

Use:

sticky mobile call/book/directions bar
phone CTA above every form
urgent pages default to call-first
exotic pages use Call About Your Pet and Book Exotic Pet Appointment
contact form as secondary, not the only path
thank-you confirmation with urgent call reminder
new patient offer lower on page, not hero-led
Form fields:

owner name
phone
email
pet species
pet name
new/existing client
preferred date
reason for visit
urgent concern checkbox
Important: do not send the pet’s medical reason into GA4 as an event parameter.

premium but approachable
mobile-first
photo-led where possible

restrained colors
