## Exotic Pet Section

**Section Heading:**  
Exotic Pet Care in Richmond, BC

**Headline:**  
Care for rabbits, reptiles, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters, hedgehogs, and small mammals.

**Copy:**  
Exotic pets often show illness differently than dogs and cats. A rabbit that stops eating, a reptile with changes in appetite or behaviour, or a small mammal that seems quieter than usual may need veterinary attention sooner than owners expect.

Francis Petcare Hospital sees a range of exotic pets, including rabbits, reptiles, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters, hedgehogs, snakes, and lizards. Our team can help with exams, health concerns, dental issues in rabbits, and surgical consultations for exotic pets when appropriate.

**Important Note:**  
Bird care is not currently listed as a confirmed service. If you are unsure whether we can see your pet, please call our team before booking.

Specialized Care for Exotic Pets

**Copy:**
